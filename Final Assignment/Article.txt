Article management system.

These are the following things you should have in application.

-> Dashboard => Dashboard will show your stats, graphs and other details.
-> User => User menu you can create menu and show all the list of users which are present on your application.
-> Category => You can add category and show the list of all the category which are present on your system.
-> Article => This menu will show you the list of article which are present on your system and along with creation of articles.

User, Article and Category in all you have to just do CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations basically.

Login Page should have normal credentials login as well as social login features.

Frontend will made from Angular JS.
Backend will made from Laravel.
